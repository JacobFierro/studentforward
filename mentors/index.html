Art & Technology Mentorship Program

Offering personal attention with a focus on post-collegiate, professional success.

The Student Forward mentorship program offers students a way to leverage the experience of successful professionals working somewhere on the art-technology spectrum to prepare for their own careers.

Long-term Personal Attention

Each student in the mentorshp program is paired with a long-term mentor that helps students navigate the rigours of college life and make smart choices early on. This mentor is with the student for the entirety of their collegiate career and is available to field more general questions as they arise.

Mentor Network

We also provide access to an amazing network of working professionals. Students can ask questions, discover unrealized opportunities and prepare for their career. These mentor sessions are spread over several years allowing the student to mature their thoughts and questions as they grow, ensuring they can reap the most from their mentor's valuable experience and insight. The mentors in this area of the program are more specifically focused on assisting the student prepare for a career in art, technology or both.

Words of Wisdom

Embrace the triumphs and disappointments all along your journey as you develop into your own true artist. Remember that there are no secrets or short-cuts that will help you achieve your goals - only hard work, patience, perseverance, awareness, education, honesty and passion.

Jorge Padilla
