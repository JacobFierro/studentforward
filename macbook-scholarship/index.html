Details

Macbook Pro

Macbooks are high-end, powerful and versatile machines. Macs are typically favored by professional creatives for their ease of use, focus on aesthetics and the wide array of creative software programs available. Equally, they are excellent for software development and can be found powering many of today's most exciting technology companies. Scholarship winners recieve a new Macbook Pro and $100 app gift card for use in college and beyond.

Programming Emphasis

The goal of Student Foward is to advance the interaction between art and technology. One way we seek to accomplish that is by promoting computer programming skills in young artists. Every scholarship winner is obliged to pursure at least 2 years of computer programming classes while in college and receives one on one tutoring help when and where they need it. The ability to code opens up huge possibilities for creative students, be it as a new facet for their artistic expression or even as a career path later in life.
